History · Where coherence begins.

Coherence originates when internal rules become non-negotiable.

Contradictions stop being explained away and start being corrected.

Early coherence is private: you do the right thing even when no one is watching.

Origins signature: fewer internal debates, faster decisions, and fewer “exceptions” you later regret.

Origin coherence occurs when constraint sets first stabilize under repeatable measurement.

The system stops optimizing locally and begins enforcing global invariants.

Early coherence is fragile due to limited feedback bandwidth and weak enforcement.

Origins signature: reduced entropy in decision outputs across similar inputs.
