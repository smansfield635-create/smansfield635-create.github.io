If you want plasma at scale, you need repeatability and controllable risk. This product targets stability envelopes, early warnings, and bounded execution.

Constraint-first plasma stability via admissibility enforcement
Plasma behavior is treated as constrained execution through admissible state regions, with early termination of inadmissible trajectories.
PROBLEM
Instability is commonly modeled as emergent noise rather than a detectable boundary violation. This delays intervention and amplifies risk under scale.
METHOD
Define admissible envelopes and treat divergence as constraint violation. Monitor coherence in motion; terminate or redirect prior to runaway.

Patterns across superficially distinct failures reduce under constraint-signature equivalence. No new physics required; missing structure restored.
