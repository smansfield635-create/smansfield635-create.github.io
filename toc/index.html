Foundational Laws

These are system laws. They define admissibility and authority boundaries.

LAW Constraint-First

  • Constraints define the feasible space before any action or narrative.
  • Outputs must be consistent with constraints or they are invalid.

LAW Quad-Core Cycle

  • Execution is modeled as: Selection → Resolution → Commitment → Continuation.
  • Continuation loops back into Selection. The cycle is lawful even when idle.

LAW Authority Separation

  • Control Room is the authoritative observer. Apps are viewers/launchers.
  • Observation does not create authority. Evidence does.

LAW No Implicit Coupling

  • No cross-engine mutation without explicit, declared operations.
  • Default stance is isolation; coupling must be lawful and visible.
Operating Principles

Principles

These guide implementation choices and publishing behavior.

PRINCIPLE Evidence-First

  • Prefer observable behavior over explanation.
  • Prefer audit-friendly outputs over persuasion.

PRINCIPLE Readable Surfaces

  • Pages must be usable on mobile without cognitive overload.
  • When in doubt: fewer controls, clearer states.

PRINCIPLE No Drift by Duplication

  • Duplicate pages create fragmentation. Consolidate into TOC until proven necessary.
  • Split into new pages only when the TOC becomes unreadable.

PRINCIPLE Compatibility-First Publishing

  • GitHub Pages is HTML/CSS/JS. Publish in compatible primitives.
  • Higher-order syntax is allowed only if the target system supports it.
