Purpose

Why this exists

The site is a traversal instrument, not a pile of disconnected pages.

Coherence

It gives structure to movement. You do not enter randomly. You orient first, then proceed by route.

Execution

Each chamber is meant to produce usable movement, not just commentary. The public surface should support that.

Verification

The architecture is repeatable. You should be able to follow a path and reach a stable result without losing the frame.

How to use

Move through the site cleanly

Start broad, then descend only where the route lawfully takes you.

1. Choose a route

Start at the door or this orientation surface. Pick the route that matches the job instead of opening everything at once.

2. Explore the surface

Use the public chamber to get a fast read. That is where products, gauges, and law become visible enough to navigate.

3. Verify the structure

A good route should stay coherent under repetition. The same path should keep producing the same class of usable output.
