1 AAI stays AAI Applied assistant intelligence, organized around the person and the pressure. Open / close
Terminology

Not generic AI language

AAI means applied assistant intelligence: intelligence organized around the person, the situation, the pressure, the missing information, and the next usable move.

Position

Practical over futuristic

The product is not trying to sound futuristic for its own sake. It is trying to make life more navigable.

Trust

Support with boundaries

AAI prepares, organizes, clarifies, compares, summarizes, questions, rehearses, and marks what still needs verification.

7 Human boundaries The boundary is part of the product. Open / close
Boundary 1

Prepare, do not impersonate

AAI can help draft, rehearse, and organize. It should not pretend to be the visitor or act as an unauthorized representative.

Boundary 2

Clarify, do not overclaim

AAI can explain possibilities and next steps. It should mark uncertainty and avoid presenting guesses as verified truth.

Boundary 3

Support, do not replace

AAI can prepare visitors for experts and institutions. It does not replace qualified professional judgment.

On Your Side AAI should not pretend to be a doctor, lawyer, therapist, financial adviser, court, employer, parent, or final authority. It is a preparation and support layer.
8 Market gap The market is full of tools. People still need someone on their side. Open / close
Gap 1

Tools without guidance

Many tools wait for perfect prompts. On Your Side AAI is framed around the visitor’s situation and helps shape the request with them.

Gap 2

Answers without preparation

A quick answer is not always enough. AAI prepares the visitor for the call, meeting, form, decision, or conversation that comes after.

Gap 3

Automation without trust

Visitors need to know where support stops. AAI stays useful by staying bounded, readable, and honest about verification.
