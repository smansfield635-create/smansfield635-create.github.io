Pricing

PLASMA · Commercial posture.

Pricing is scope-based. PLASMA is offered as an execution engagement or a licensed workflow package.

  • Explore — prove value quickly with one branch and a minimal receipt set.
  • Build — implement receipt schemas, invalidation rules, and exportable audit format.
  • Scale — multi-branch execution, observer agreement, and partner-ready delivery packs.
What changes the price

Branch count, receipt complexity, number of stakeholders/observers, and integration/export requirements.

What stays constant

Receipts remain required. No receipts means no advancement, regardless of scope.

Pricing scales with measurable complexity units:

  • Topology: number of branches and dependencies.
  • Receipts: schema count, falsifiability requirements, invalidation graph.
  • Observers: review roles, auditability demands, agreement targets.
  • Interfaces: integration surfaces and export formats.
Scope unit (practical)

A “branch set” with defined receipts and a validated export trail.

Outcome coupling

As scope rises, acceptance thresholds should be tied to recurrence reduction and correction latency reduction.
