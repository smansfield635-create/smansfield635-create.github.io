Recognition

Wayfinding value

Recognition is the first gate. A visitor does not need to understand the whole estate before beginning. The first task is to notice which star carries the next useful question.

Coherence

Coherence asks whether the required parts of a person, system, world, or decision can hold together under pressure.

Wayfinding Marker · The Minimum Principle Sd = min(Ed, Id, Vd)

Practical Lens: the decisive question is often not “Where am I strongest?” but “Which required capacity is closest to failing?”

Engineering Lens: the coherence spine is noncompensatory. The system state is governed by the weakest required axis, not by an average of all axes.

Measurement

Measurement gives intuition a surface. It does not replace judgment; it gives judgment something stable enough to inspect.
