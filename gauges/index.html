Known gap classes

Route identity failure. The wrong page or wrong body is served from a route. Example: Earth URL serving Hearth route authority.
Visible receipt spillover. The route is probably functioning, but visitor-facing text still exposes contract names, source markers, or build-language spillover.
Route proof hold. The page framing is acceptable, but the board cannot yet see live route-readable proof for the active canvas, hydration, runtime, or gauge state.
Audit-rule mismatch. The page may be coherent, but the audit rule needs broader route-family detection instead of exact phrase dependency.
