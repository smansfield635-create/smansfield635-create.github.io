Coherence · Where it appears across time.

Core idea

Coherence shows up anywhere a system must remain stable under pressure. It is the shared pattern behind reliable behavior, repeatable outcomes, and trust that compounds.

Where it appears

  • Science: reproducibility and consistency across experiments.
  • Institutions: rules that remain the same regardless of who is watching.
  • People: alignment between words, actions, and outcomes over time.

Why it matters

When coherence exists, systems can scale without force. When it collapses, systems require control, enforcement, and narrative patching.
