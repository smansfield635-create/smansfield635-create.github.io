Coherence · what it is.

Intro

Coherence means you are one person in every room. Your rules don’t change when pressure changes.

Four paragraphs

Definition: your values, actions, and outcomes match.

Mechanism: you close gaps fast instead of defending contradictions.

Measurement: others can predict your behavior from your stated standards.

Examples: trust grows; drama shrinks; decisions stay clean under stress.
