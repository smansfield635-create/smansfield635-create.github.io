Planet origin

The world was not found thriving. It was found waiting.

Planet 1 was found desolate and in despair. The point is not conquest. The point is restoration.

At the center is a floating magnetic core: half natural, half man-made. The natural half carries water, terrain, fruit, seed, memory, and life. The man-made half carries structure, measurement, city systems, repair, instruments, and access.
