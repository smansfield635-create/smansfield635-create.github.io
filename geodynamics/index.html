Geodynamics

The dynamics of coherence across time, pressure, and scale.

Equation

Minimal structural form (domain-agnostic):

C(t) = 1 / (1 + D(t))
D(t) = || y(t) − x(t) || / max(ε, || x(t) ||)
ΔC/Δt = C(t+Δt) − C(t)

Where x(t) is the intended/declared state, y(t) is the observed state, D(t) is normalized divergence, and C(t) is coherence in [0,1].

Scientific definition

Geodynamics is the study of how coherence evolves as a constrained trajectory: changes in admissibility, evidence, latency, and receipts determine the allowable motion of a system through state-space.

Human explanation

Coherence is what “still lines up” when life applies pressure. Geodynamics is the map of how that lining-up strengthens, weakens, drifts, and re-stabilizes over time.

Seven Invariant Constraints

Show / hide
  • Coherence Constraint: outputs cannot remain aligned beyond the alignment of inputs under pressure.
  • Evidence Constraint: claims cannot outrun verifiable evidence without collapse.
  • Feedback Latency Constraint: delayed feedback amplifies instability non-linearly.
  • Conservation of Load: unresolved load does not disappear; it redistributes.
  • Boundary Integrity Constraint: boundaries inconsistently enforced cease to function.
  • Selection Pressure Constraint: systems evolve toward what is selected, not what is stated.
  • Receipts Constraint: systems cannot learn or converge without persistent records of decisions and outcomes.

Foundational Tiers

Tier 1 — Derived Laws
  • Coherence cannot exceed evidence.
  • Outcomes cannot exceed input coherence.
  • Constraints define admissibility.
  • No receipt → no defensible claim.
Tier 2 — Failure Dynamics
  • Pressure reveals structure.
  • Unobserved drift accumulates.
  • Exceptions dissolve rules.
  • Lag follows drag; slack follows lag.
Tier 3 — Observable Signals
  • Drift: plans, behavior, outcomes diverge over time.
  • Rule failure: boundaries exist but don’t constrain.
  • Promise failure: decisions are made without follow-through.
  • Metric conflict: different measurements tell incompatible stories.

Engine mapping

Show / hide
  • Selection: Evidence + Selection Pressure (what enters, why it was admissible).
  • Resolution: Boundary Integrity (what constraints bind, what conflicts resolve).
  • Commitment: Coherence (what is locked, what is promised).
  • Continuation: Receipts + Latency + Load (what persists, what drifts, what is verified).
