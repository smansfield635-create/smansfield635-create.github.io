Door rule

Perspective is chosen here.

The Door does not replace the Compass. The Compass routes. The Door chooses perspective. Once a visitor chooses Flat, Round, or Globe, the Door carries that view forward into the next page.

Flat: direct route, minimal framing, fastest public read.
Round: connected route, relationship between rooms, moderate context.
Globe: full-system route, whole-house orientation, highest context.
