Definitions
Condensed reference terms. No interpretation enforcement.
Coherence tap
Coherence is alignment across claims, actions, and outcomes under constraints.
  • External trust cannot exceed internal self-honesty/self-respect.
  • Coherence is observable via consistency under load.
Fragmentation tap
Fragmentation is misalignment between stated values, actions, and outcomes.
  • Structural signal, not a narrative judgment.
  • Amplifies instability when paired with confusion.
Confusion tap
Confusion is unresolved coordinate assignment that accelerates instability once present.
  • Not required for instability, but increases rate of failure.
  • Increases interpretive variance between observers.
Surface discipline tap
A surface is a constrained context separating authority, observation, and execution.
  • Control is observer-only.
  • Artifacts are interactive without durable state mutation.
  • Execution remains disabled unless explicitly enabled by versioned release.
