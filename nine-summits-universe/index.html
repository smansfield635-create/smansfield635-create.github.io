World origin

The planet was found desolate and in despair.

This world was not discovered in a thriving state. It was found dormant, damaged, and spiritually exhausted: a planet with immense buried value, but without stable continuity. The first planetary story is restoration, not conquest.

The restoration begins with the floating magnetic core. The natural half carries life, memory, terrain, seed, water, fruit, ecology, and organic possibility. The man-made half carries structure, routing, measurement, city systems, repair, instruments, and access control.

Natural half

Life and memory.

The natural half carries terrain, water, fruit, organic recovery, and the planet’s deep ecological memory.

Man-made half

Structure and repair.

The man-made half carries measurement, routing, city order, technology, access grammar, and repair systems.
