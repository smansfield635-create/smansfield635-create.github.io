Industrial Posture (C)

Public-energy posture default. Industrial substitution framing only.

Rule

All public energy statements default to Industrial (C): substitution and efficiency posture, not net-gain claims.

Constraint

Fusion is treated as readiness doctrine only. No net-positive gain language. No “achieved output” language.

Operational meaning

If a sentence cannot be defended under Industrial (C), it is rewritten or removed before publication.

Bindings

These links route to implementation surfaces. They are not part of doctrine itself.
