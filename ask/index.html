What this is

This assistant explains concepts you have already encountered while navigating the system.

It does not suggest paths, unlock routes, or replace understanding. The geometry remains authoritative.

What you can ask

  • Definitions of terms you saw
  • Why a distinction exists
  • How two concepts relate
  • How a traversal can be interpreted

What you cannot ask

  • Where to go next
  • What you should choose
  • How to bypass the gate
  • What the “correct” path is

Authority order

Geometry > Path > Observation > Explanation

If an explanation conflicts with the geometry, the explanation is wrong.
