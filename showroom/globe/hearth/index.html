Narrative Construct

Hearth is future Earth surviving long enough to become shelter.

Hearth is not a generic planet. It is a future-Earth survival chamber: a technically advanced world, roughly forty billion years forward, where pressure, scars, hydrology, materials, and shelter systems show what could remain if Earth survived instead of collapsed.

ZIONTS shows consequence after living conditions fail. H-Earth studies the pressure conditions that survival must face. Audralia shows untouched living possibility. Hearth is the conversion room: survival pressure becoming a place where people can actually stay.

The engineering layer remains a proof layer. The visible cockpit is diagnostic equipment. The planet carrier remains canvas-owned. The source stack remains held.
