Five Flags: What’s My Scene?

A family-friendly guessing game for ages 8 and up. One player knows the scene, but cannot say it. They can only guide the room with five flags while everyone else tries to guess what is happening.

It is easy to learn: pick a card, raise a flag, guess the scene. The fun is watching the room read the flags, miss, turn, get warmer, and finally nail it.
