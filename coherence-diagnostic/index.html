Coherence Diagnostic Assessment

This assessment compares how coherent you believe yourself to be, which archetype you claim, and what your scenario choices reveal under pressure.

12 questions minimum Q1 fixed scale Q2 fixed archetype claim Primary + secondary choices Local-only scoring No storage
This assessment is not a medical, mental-health, legal, or employment diagnostic. It is a self-reflection and pattern-assessment tool for coherence, consistency, accountability, and decision alignment.

Assessment Mode

Choose how you want the result handled.

Graded mode shows a score, band, calibration gap, and archetype match. Ungraded mode gives a neutral pattern read without a formal score.

Question 1 · Fixed Intake Anchor

On a scale from 1 to 10, how coherent do you believe yourself to be?

This is a self-perception anchor. It does not prove the final score by itself.

Question 2 · Fixed Claim Anchor

Read the four archetypes. Which archetype do you believe best describes you?

The claimed archetype is a claim anchor, not proof. The assessment compares it against your scenario pattern.

Questions 3–12 · Diagnostic Scenarios

Choose a primary and secondary response for each scenario.

Primary means what you would do first. Secondary means what you would rely on next. The same option cannot be selected twice for the same question.

Answers are processed locally in this browser. This MVP does not store or submit results.
