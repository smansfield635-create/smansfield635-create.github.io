Why the separation matters

Financial problems become harder to resolve when contract meaning, receivable recognition, payable execution, and allocation logic are treated as one undifferentiated process.

ARCHCOIN identifies the domain that owns the question. Each domain then opens four rooms: Overview, Engineering, Platform, and Governance.
