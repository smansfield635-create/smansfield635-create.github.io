Benchmarks

PLASMA · What we measure to prove it works.

Benchmarks answer one question: does PLASMA reduce waste while increasing clarity?

  • Fewer circular debates and fewer stalled loops.
  • Faster convergence to an admissible next step.
  • Fewer regressions caused by hidden substitutions.
  • Cleaner handoffs between people and teams.
Minimum viable proof

Show one complete execution path where each step has a receipt, and an independent reviewer agrees the transitions were admissible.

What “better” looks like

Less time spent arguing about reality, fewer reversals, and faster repair when assumptions change.

Benchmark classes:

  • Correction latency: time from deviation detection to executed repair.
  • Recurrence rate: frequency of the same failure class repeating.
  • Observer agreement: independent classification match rate of state validity.
  • Variance bounds: output stability under comparable inputs and constraints.
Receipt integrity

Receipts must be explicit, inspectable, and falsifiable. If a receipt cannot be independently checked, it does not count.

Acceptance posture

Thresholds tighten only after new evidence classes are observed; otherwise, only time passage reduces uncertainty.
