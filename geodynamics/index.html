Geodynamics

The mechanics of coherence across structure, interpretation, and experience.

Scientific formulation

Coherence(t) = 1 / (1 + Divergence(t))

Divergence describes misalignment between intention, action, and outcome under constraint and pressure.

Scientific definition

Geodynamics is the observer-invariant study of how coherence evolves as systems move through time, pressure, and constraint.

It describes how alignment is maintained, lost, and restored.

Human translation

When things feel confusing, signals stop lining up.

When coherence returns, reality becomes checkable again: what happened, what was allowed, and what proved out.

Where this fits
  • Laws describe what cannot be violated.
  • Principles describe how laws appear in life.
  • Geodynamics describes how coherence moves between them.
  • State is the lived result.
