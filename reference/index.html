Core Terms

  • Coherence — sustained lawful progression without contradiction or fragmentation.
  • Cycle — Selection → Resolution → Commitment → Continuation.
  • Fuel — coherence reserve; full unless burnout or fragmentation occurs.
  • RPM — activity density over time.
  • Speed — net forward progression in the cycle.
  • Risk — instability derived from fragmentation and wall states.

Invariants

  • No manual advancement is required for coherence.
  • Breakage produces signal; normal operation produces silence.
  • Authority lives in the Control Room.
  • Apps are viewers only.
