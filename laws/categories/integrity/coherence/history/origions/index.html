Origins

History · Where coherence begins.

Coherence originates when an individual stops negotiating with themselves. Internal rules become non-optional.

This moment usually follows failure, loss, or exposure of contradiction.

Origin coherence is private, quiet, and easily broken if not protected.

Its signature is relief: cognitive load drops because decisions stop conflicting.

Origin coherence occurs when constraint sets are first stabilized.

The system ceases to optimize locally and begins enforcing global invariants.

Early coherence is fragile due to limited feedback bandwidth.

Signature: reduced entropy in decision outputs.
