Consistency

Alignment across time.

Consistency is the first visible sign of integrity. It is the condition where actions, claims, and outcomes remain aligned regardless of pressure, audience, or circumstance.

When behavior shifts but values do not, integrity fractures. When values shift to justify behavior, integrity collapses.

In nature, consistency allows ecosystems to stabilize. In systems, it allows trust to form. In people, it allows credibility to exist.

Same inputs → same outputs.
