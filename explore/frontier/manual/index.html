Manual

Instructions should make the next move obvious.

Manual turns information into usable action. It tells the visitor what to read, what to decide, what to do, what to check, where to return, and when to revise.

Operating Flow

The useful question is not “where are the instructions?” It is “what do I do next?”

Read What needs to be understood first?
Choose Which path or mode applies?
Act What is the next executable step?
Check What confirms the step worked?
Return Where does the user go back?
Revise What changes if the check fails?
Repeat What becomes the next cycle?
