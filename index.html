Perhaps the next step is not abandoning what came before, but arranging it differently.

What if the same is true of the problems we face now? What if many of our greatest challenges are not waiting for more information, but for another way of organizing what we already possess?

What if the next frontier is not outside the box, but outside the cube?

Ground-Up Build

One person carried the architecture, code, research expression, navigation, diagnostics, worlds, and visual direction into one connected estate.

HTML carries structure. CSS carries atmosphere and responsive presentation. JavaScript carries state, navigation, interaction, and lifecycle control. Canvas and WebGL carry the living visual systems.

Nothing was adapted from a commercial theme or assembled from prefabricated page systems.
