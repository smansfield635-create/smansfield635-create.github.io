Act I: Symptoms

Symptoms are signals.
They tell you something is off — not why.

Confusion, frustration, instability — these are not failures. They are felt evidence that alignment has begun to slip.

Common signals

  • Drift — plans, behavior, and outcomes slowly diverge.
  • Rule fatigue — boundaries exist but don’t constrain.
  • Promise decay — decisions are made without follow-through.

Symptoms belong here. Causes come later.
