Where it appears

Coherence shows up anywhere parts act like one system instead of fighting each other. You can spot it in physics, biology, teams, institutions, and personal decision-making.

Fast examples

  • Nature: stable ecosystems, feedback loops, homeostasis.
  • Systems: reliable outputs under stress and change.
  • People: values, actions, and outcomes staying aligned over time.
