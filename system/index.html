Foundation
No Hidden Control
If something matters, it must be visible. No silent changes.
Principles
Simple tests
1) Does it match what it claims? 2) Does it hold under pressure? 3) Can anyone verify it?
Definitions
Coherence
Things match: what you say, what you do, and what happens.
Fragmentation
Things don’t match. Misalignment grows under pressure.
Confusion
You can’t tell what’s true or what to do. That makes failure faster.
