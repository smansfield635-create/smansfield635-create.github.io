Laws

Invariant constraints that hold whether believed or not.

Law 1 — Reality binds behavior

What is true constrains outcomes regardless of intention.

Law 2 — Misalignment creates cost

When values, actions, and results diverge, correction becomes unavoidable.

Law 3 — Boundaries reduce harm

Clear limits prevent small errors from becoming systemic failure.

Law 4 — Shortcuts accumulate debt

Unresolved compromises surface later with greater force.

Law 5 — Pressure reveals structure

Stress does not change systems; it exposes them.

Law 6 — Execution requires follow-through

Decisions without completion are indistinguishable from avoidance.

Law 7 — Coherence precedes scale

Growth amplifies structure; it does not fix it.
