Narrative compartment · First frame

Audralia lets us simulate what these frontier sciences are reaching toward.

Audralia may model bounded possibilities, dramatize future-world consequences, test public understanding, and show Energy, Water, Agriculture, Infrastructure, Governance, and other frontier sciences interacting inside a living world.
Simulation World Audralia is not proof. It is the sandbox. It can model scenarios, pressures, tradeoffs, failures, and frontier systems without turning conjecture into validated deployment.
Dream Layer The world may dream forward. Energy deserts become storage fields. Rural land becomes clean-energy reserve ground. Storage vaults become civilization anchors.
Fusion Horizon Not magic. Pressure. Fusion remains a horizon that forces the crew to master storage, load discipline, infrastructure, and timing before the breakthrough arrives.
Simulation boundary What Audralia may and may not do.
May Do Model bounded possibility. Audralia may dramatize advanced buffers, settlement risk, rural storage fields, future offtake pressure, and civilization-building choices.
May Not Do Override evidence. Audralia may not be treated as proof of deployment, validated commercial fusion, direct solar-to-fusion, risk-free energy, or consumer high-energy endpoints.

Science compartment

What is scientifically true, what is not ready, and what must not be claimed?

Science Fusion is real. Controlled fusion requires plasma. Plasma must be heated, confined, stabilized, and sustained. Commercial plant-level electricity is not procurement-ready.
Math Gain types must be separated. Q_target, Q_plasma, and Q_plant are not the same. Commercial viability requires sustainable Q_plant greater than 1 after full facility losses.
Possibility Preparation is valid now. Solar is a hedge. Storage is a bridge. Fusion is frontier readiness. High-load businesses can prepare now.
Science boundary Open claim limits.
  • No “solar becomes fusion.”
  • No commercial fusion now.
  • No plasma battery solution.
  • No unlimited power.
  • No fusion procurement today.
  • No plant-level commercial readiness without sustainable Q_plant > 1 after total facility losses.
