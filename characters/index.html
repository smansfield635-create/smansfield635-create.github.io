Perspective Series · Mirrorland

Shadows Never Shatter in Mirrorland.

The anomaly opened. The return path never did.

Dextrion remains on Earth at the public Mirror Manor. From there, he detects the Bermuda-area anomaly and sends the first team through. The crossing works. Coming back does not.

Mirrorland is real on the other side. Inside Mirrorland is Audralia. The one-way teleport can send builders, engineers, protectors, field operators, and survival crews in, but it cannot bring them home. They build the manor. They build the next plan. The story begins after the manor already stands.

The clock is set at 300 years. Another planetary transition is coming. If the technology is ready, Mirrorland and Earth can survive. If it is not, annihilation remains possible. Mirrorland’s rules also apply to Earth.
