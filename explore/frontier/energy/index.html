Science

What is true, what is not ready, and what must not be claimed?

Science preserves the proof boundary beneath the narrative world and product catalog.

Fusion Fusion is real. Controlled fusion requires plasma. Plasma must be heated, confined, stabilized, and sustained.
Commercial Status Not procurement-ready. Commercial fusion electricity is not procurement-ready. Plant-level net electricity has not yet been achieved.
Readiness Preparation is valid now. Solar is a hedge. Storage is a bridge. Fusion is frontier readiness.
Open math boundary
Q_target Target gain. Fusion energy from target divided by energy delivered to target.
Q_plasma Plasma gain. Fusion thermal power divided by external plasma heating power.
Q_plant Plant gain. Net electric output divided by total facility energy input.
Q_target, Q_plasma, and Q_plant are separate. Commercial viability requires sustainable Q_plant greater than 1 after total facility inputs, losses, reset costs, maintenance, conversion, and reliability burdens.
