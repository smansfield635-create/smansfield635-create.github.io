Geodynamics

Free will first. Structure second. Mechanism last.

Scientific equation

ΔC = f(E, K, R)  with gate: C ≤ E

C = coherence, E = evidence, K = constraints, R = receipts.

Scientific definition

Geodynamics is the observer-invariant study of how coherence is constrained under pressure: claims, actions, and outcomes remain admissible only when bounded by evidence, constraints, and verification.

Human explanation

When reality stops being checkable, confusion grows. Coherence returns when you can point to what happened, what was allowed, and what proved out.
