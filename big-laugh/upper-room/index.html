Ticket Rules How to earn raffle tickets.

Pre-register, show up, bring friends, attend shows, and stay until the end.

Raffle tickets can be earned several ways.

  • Pre-register: receive 5 tickets.
  • Show up: receive 1 additional ticket.
  • Bring a friend: receive 1 additional ticket per friend on that friend’s first qualifying enrollment or attendance.
  • Attend each show: receive 1 ticket per show attended.
  • Stay until the end: receive 1 final ticket when leaving.
Official raffle rules, eligibility, timing, and prize details will be posted with the event schedule.
