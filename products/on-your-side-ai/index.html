How It Works at a High Level

The product flow is diagnostic, not mystical. It moves from what can be observed to what can be understood to what can be acted on more cleanly.

1. Gather

Take in the relevant behavioral and contextual signals.

2. Organize

Sort those signals into a clearer pattern instead of leaving them fragmented.

3. Clarify

Reduce confusion so the decision surface becomes more intelligible.

4. Support Action

Help the user move with more reliable and better-aligned execution.
