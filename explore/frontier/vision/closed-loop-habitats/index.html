Why It Exists

Closed Loop Habitats are the extreme case: no cheap resupply, no waste export, and no forgiveness for drift. Any system that survives here can scale down to Earth.

Closed-Loop Requirement

Water, air, food, materials, and energy are treated as a single coupled inventory. The habitat is a constraint-locked refinery: every stream is measured, routed, and recovered.

SHIMMER Bridge

SHIMMER functions as the entropy-routing backbone: classification → segmentation → dynamic routing → conversion → retention. The same routing logic supports both city-scale recovery and habitat-scale survival.
