Methods

PRIORITY KNOWLEDGE BOUNDARY. All pre-verbal, sensory, and intuitive inputs are treated as priority knowledge—providing access to signal without conferring authority. Such inputs may inform exploration but may not override externally observable evidence, invariant structure, or proof-gated execution.

METHODS PAGE ROLE. This page presents only the highest-signal empirical findings. Detailed mechanisms, diagnostics, instrumentation mechanics, and stress narratives are intentionally deferred to branch pages.

Tier 1 · Foundational

CONSTRAINT-FIRST EXECUTION GENERATES STRUCTURE. Coherent execution systems emerge when constraints are enforced before optimization, tooling, or narrative framing. Architecture appears as a consequence—not a design target.

Tier 2 · Core Mechanisms

ENGINE COHERENCE PRECEDES SPECIALIZATION. Unified execution can subsume hosting, logic, validation, search, and interaction when closure and proof rules are enforced globally.

INSTRUMENTATION ENABLES DIAGNOSTICS. Execution is only diagnosable when state, activity, and transitions are explicitly recorded.

Tier 3 · Derived Implications

STRESS IS A VALID PROOF SURFACE. Successful construction under severe constraints—single device, cellular network, compressed time—carries greater evidenti weight than idealized builds.

COLLAPSE IS A SIGNAL. Architectural collapse indicates successful constraint convergence when invariants remain preserved.

FAILURE MODES ARE DATA. Failures that do not violate invariants define system boundaries and are admissible diagnostic evidence.
