ESL Traversal Learning

English by movement, not overload.

The classroom does not bury the learner in grammar. It moves from scene to listening, meaning, speaking, and use.

This page is now grounded inside the Manor as a classroom: compact, visual, and built to show the learning path without turning into a long legacy scroll.
