Act II: Diagnosis

Diagnosis separates what you feel from what is structurally failing.

Structural failure patterns
  • Misalignment — stated values, actions, and outcomes disagree.
  • Boundary collapse — constraints exist but don’t bind behavior.
  • Follow-through failure — decisions do not produce receipts.

Diagnosis does not assign blame. It identifies where coherence breaks.
