Missing constraints cannot be offset. No shortcuts. Requirements do not trade.

L6 · Non-Compensation Law Non-compensation

Strength in one dimension cannot compensate for violation in another required constraint. If a requirement is missing, extra strength somewhere else doesn’t “make up” for it.

L7 · No-Timer Gating Law No timer gating

Time passage cannot substitute for structural completion, receipt, or constraint satisfaction. Waiting doesn’t complete the work. Completion requires the missing step.
