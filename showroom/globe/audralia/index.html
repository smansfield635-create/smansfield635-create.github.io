Audralia Conservatory

About This Conservatory

The Audralia Conservatory is a protected planetary holder and viewing environment. It preserves a stable place for Audralia while the Hearth Development R&D Chamber develops the reusable construction engine that will later build and deliver the fuller planetary system.

The planet currently presented here is provisional. This page is prepared to receive the later canonical planet, but the planetary construction engine is not running inside the Conservatory.
