Opening rule

The metaverse opens when the path becomes a place.

A lesson becomes a room. A wound becomes a gate. A climb becomes a world.

01 Start with the human path: character, structure, balance, stability, peace, joy, dignity, free will, and love.
02 Enter the planet: seven landmasses, harder terrain, sweeter fruit, and deeper solutions.
03 Move by coherence. The world does not open by accident. It opens by readiness.
04 Return with proof: a story, a product, a map, a room, a show, or a usable tool.

Planet origin

The world was not found thriving. It was found waiting.

Planet 1 was found desolate and in despair. The point is not conquest. The point is restoration.

At the center is a floating magnetic core: half natural, half man-made. The natural half carries water, terrain, fruit, seed, memory, and life. The man-made half carries structure, measurement, city systems, repair, instruments, and access.

Natural half

Life remembers.

The planet still carries seed, water, minerals, weather, memory, and organic recovery.

Constructed half

Structure repairs.

The world needs instruments, cities, routes, gates, measurements, and tools to become livable again.

How access works

The higher the coherence, the harder the terrain.

The world does not get easier as you rise. It gets more worth reaching.

Entry terrain

Stable ground.

Simple routes, clear signs, basic cities, ordinary food, and safe first movement.

Developing terrain

The first climb.

Regional movement, stronger lessons, harder choices, richer fruit, and clearer self-knowledge.

High terrain

The rare path.

Severe mountains, advanced cities, rare materials, deep comprehension, and sweeter solutions.

Peak terrain

The summit field.

Polar access, diamond ice, deep-core gates, final trials, and the cleanest answers.

Seven landmasses

The world has one Mainland, four major regions, and two poles.

Landmass 1

The Mainland

The first-entry civilization field: countries, roads, cities, public rooms, schools, markets, and the beginning of the climb.

Material profile: granite, marble, copper, iron, gold, and platinum.

Landmass 2

North Main Region

Law, clarity, memory, discipline, cold pressure, summit trials, and structural academies.

Material profile: diamond, slate, platinum, and iron.

Landmass 3

East Main Region

Learning, invention, exploration, language, schools, discovery paths, and luminous experimental cities.

Material profile: opal, copper, silver, and marble.

Landmass 4

South Main Region

Restoration, agriculture, food systems, gardens, care, family continuity, and recovery corridors.

Material profile: granite, marble, copper, gold, and opal.

Landmass 5

West Main Region

Trial, fracture, defense, proof, repair after pressure, severe cliffs, slate fields, and metallic fault lines.

Material profile: slate, iron, lead, silver, and diamond.

Landmass 6

North Pole

Extreme upper-coherence access: diamond ice, magnetic skyfields, upper-world gates, and final law trials.

Material profile: diamond, platinum, iron, and slate.

Landmass 7

South Pole

Extreme depth-coherence access: pressure fields, hidden caverns, root-world gates, survival trials, and deep-core restoration.

Material profile: granite, opal, lead, gold, and copper.

Harder terrain means the world is asking for higher coherence. The reward is deeper comprehension, sweeter fruit, and more elegant possibility.
