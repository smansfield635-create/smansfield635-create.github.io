Fusion Readiness Rule

Requirements-only doctrine. No gain claims. No “achieved output” language.

Rule

Fusion is expressed only as readiness prerequisites. This site does not claim net-positive gain, net electric output, or production achievement.

Readiness channels

If a statement implies achievement, it is rewritten into a requirement or removed.

  • Instrumentation baseline and calibration traceability
  • Receipt-first evidence chain
  • Explicit falsifiers and scope locks
  • Independent replication protocol
  • Claim tier separation: concept vs measurement

Operational check

If you cannot point to a measured receipt that survives the admissibility rules, the claim stays at “readiness only.”

Bindings

These links route to implementation surfaces. They are not part of doctrine itself.
