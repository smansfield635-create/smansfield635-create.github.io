How coherence changes over time — without opinion, without pressure.

Same meaning. Only the language changes.

Human translation

When life feels confusing, it usually means signals stopped lining up. Coherence returns when reality becomes checkable again: what happened, what was allowed, and what proved out.

  • Drift is when what you say, do, and get stop matching.
  • Pressure reveals what is real and what was only assumed.
  • Small corrections early prevent large collapses later.

Scientific language

Coherence can be expressed as the inverse of divergence between declared state and observed state. This is a structural description, not a moral claim.

Mechanics
Coherence(t) = 1 / (1 + Divergence(t))
Divergence reflects misalignment between intention, action, and outcome.
Interpretation
  • Lower divergence → higher coherence → behavior remains predictable under stress.
  • Higher divergence → lower coherence → contradictions persist and systems destabilize.
  • Receipts are evidence that transitions occurred as claimed.
