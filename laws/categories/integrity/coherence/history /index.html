Coherence

History · Where it appears.

Where it shows up

Coherence is the pattern where the story, the behavior, and the outcomes keep matching over time—across pressure, audience, and circumstance. When those drift apart, the system loses coherence.

Canonical laws in this branch

  • Coherence Constraint Law — external coherence cannot exceed internal self-coherence.
  • Fragmentation Detection Law — misalignment between stated values, executed actions, and produced outcomes indicates loss of coherence.
