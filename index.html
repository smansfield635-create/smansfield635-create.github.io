About

Origin. I am a builder-philosopher drawn to philosophy, theology, and science since childhood. I came from instability and learned self-cultivation through repeated real-world collapse and repair.

Inflection. On a solitary Christmas, an empirical question landed: human behavior appears to follow constraints like physical systems. I did not know “constraints,” “canonical,” or “social thermodynamics.” I started executing and letting evidence steer.

China. Time in China and deep exposure to Chinese culture—Laozi, Mengzi, Zhuangzi, Hanfeizi, Bingfa, Confucius—reshaped my question-asking. Mandarin study and lived contrast sharpened my focus on structure, boundaries, and coherence.

Present. Born Oct 1, 1985 in Grand Prairie, Texas. I currently sell LeafGuard gutters. This work is independent: it documents execution, artifacts, and limits—no credential claims, no persuasion, no intent inference.
