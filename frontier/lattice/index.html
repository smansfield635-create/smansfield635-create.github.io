What Lattice owns

Lattice owns grid logic, state-space organization, structural mapping, field relationships, and the language needed to describe how a Frontier system is organized before it is built.

What Lattice does not own

Lattice does not own the root command surface, the product wing, Showroom proof, Gauges logic, Earth G1 rendering, heavy runtime, or unrelated Frontier lanes.
