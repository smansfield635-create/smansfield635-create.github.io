PC5 · Baseline Nutrition Systems · Kitchen / Pantry

Food is support. Not a moral score.

Water, food, timing, rhythm, recovery, and repeatable support before optimization ever enters the room.

Baseline Nutrition Systems helps people return to the basics without turning food into shame. The first goal is not aesthetic transformation. The first goal is stability: fewer crash days, fewer skipped basics, fewer chaotic decisions, and more steady rhythm.

The kitchen does not shame the body. It brings the day back into range.
Most people do not need a perfect diet. They need a survivable baseline.
Optimization fails when the basics are missing.

6 Human boundaries Nutrition support is not medical care. Open / close
Support, not shame The product should never make food a moral score. It should help visitors return to support.
Organize, not prescribe The product can organize habits and options. It should not prescribe medical diets.
Refer when needed Medical conditions, eating disorders, pregnancy concerns, chronic symptoms, or specialized needs require qualified professional care.
Baseline Nutrition Systems is a practical support product. It does not diagnose, prescribe, treat, or replace qualified medical, nutrition, or mental-health support.
