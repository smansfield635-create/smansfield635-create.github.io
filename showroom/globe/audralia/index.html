Untouched Living Path

What to look for here

A living-world contrast. Audralia does not begin with collapse. It begins with the possibility that the planet was supported instead of wounded.
Material becoming. This path should feel alive, restored, distinct, and constructive. It carries the question of what beauty can become when damage is not the governing force.
Stewardship as structure. The future here is not escape from Earth. It is an imagined world where care, restraint, and support shape the conditions of life.
Its own identity. Audralia remains its own constructed living path. It does not borrow ZIONTS’ warning role or H-Earth’s ground-level survival lens.
Audralia answers the Globe question with stewardship: support instead of damage, becoming instead of collapse.
