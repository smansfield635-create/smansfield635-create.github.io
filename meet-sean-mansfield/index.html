Sean had known adversity for most of his life. The real turn came when he stopped seeing every obstacle as only outside of him and started recognizing where choices, reactions, habits, and silence were keeping the cycle alive.

Adversity The pressure was real. The page names it without turning the profile into a hardship inventory.
