Laws

Same laws. Two lenses. Driven by fact, not fiction.

The meaning is identical. Only the language changes.

Human language

Law 1 — Reality can’t be faked
You can claim anything. Reality only responds to what’s true.
Law 2 — Trust begins internally
External trust cannot exceed internal honesty.
Law 3 — Outputs reflect inputs
Outcomes cannot be more coherent than their premises.
Law 4 — Constraints define admissibility
What’s allowed decides what can happen.
Law 5 — Verification gates reality
Receipts separate story from proof.
Law 6 — Confusion accelerates failure
Once incoherence exists, confusion speeds collapse.
Law 7 — Receipts enable convergence
Verified steps stabilize systems and let them scale.

These are constraint statements: what holds whether acknowledged or not.

Scientific language

  1. Evidence bound: Coherence cannot exceed evidence.
  2. Internal bound: External trust cannot exceed internal honesty.
  3. Input bound: Outcomes cannot be more coherent than inputs.
  4. Admissibility: Constraints define admissible states/transitions.
  5. Verification: Verification gates reality.
  6. Confusion acceleration: Confusion accelerates failure once incoherence exists.
  7. Receipt convergence: Receipts enable convergence.
