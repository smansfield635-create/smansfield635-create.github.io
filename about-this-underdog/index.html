Zone 2 · The Turning Point Adversity → accountability → redirection.

Adversity revealed the pattern. Sean had known pressure for most of his life, but the real shift came when he stopped treating every obstacle as something outside of him. He began to see where his own choices, reactions, habits, and silence were helping the cycle continue.

Accountability changed the direction. Once Sean could admit his part without collapsing into shame, he could finally switch gears. That shift became the foundation of the voice behind This Underdog: pressure can become language, language can become a map, and a map can become a way out before it is too late.
