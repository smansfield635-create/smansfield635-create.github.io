You Are Here Because Something Feels Off

Systems drift. Teams drift. Products drift. Language drifts. And when drift compounds, it stops being cosmetic — it becomes structural.

Most failures do not begin with catastrophe. They begin with invisible misalignment. Small inconsistencies. Quiet contradictions. Shared exposure nobody mapped.

This project exists because drift is not random. It can be measured. It can be bounded. It can be expressed within a finite lattice.

We do not operate on vibes. We operate on declared scope, receipts, invariants, and replayable structure.

If you are here, there is a question. A system. A structure. A pressure point. And somewhere in the lattice, there is an answer.
