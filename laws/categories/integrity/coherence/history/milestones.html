Milestones

History · Observable thresholds of coherence.

Milestones are behavioral locks, not emotional moments.

A milestone occurs when fewer exceptions are required to maintain trust.

A milestone occurs when repair happens quickly and publicly, without blame-shifting.

A milestone occurs when standards remain stable under profit pressure and social pressure.

Milestones are visible because they reduce negotiation, explanation, and corrective overhead.

CANONICAL LAWS (COHERENCE)

  • Coherence Constraint Law — external coherence cannot exceed internal self-coherence.
  • Fragmentation Detection Law — misalignment between stated values, executed actions, and produced outcomes indicates loss of coherence.
