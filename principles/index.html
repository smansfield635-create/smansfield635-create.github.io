Principles

How laws appear in lived reality.

Tier 1 — What must hold

What is true stays true under pressure. What isn’t eventually breaks.

  • Honesty stabilizes decisions.
  • Boundaries reduce harm.
  • Inputs shape outcomes.
Tier 2 — How coherence is maintained
  • Define what counts before you act.
  • Make constraints explicit, not implied.
  • Require receipts for irreversible steps.
Tier 3 — What you notice when coherence shifts
  • Drift increases when words, actions, and outcomes stop matching.
  • Confusion rises when rules become optional.
  • Stability returns when reality becomes checkable again.
