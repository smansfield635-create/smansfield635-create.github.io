Classification & Stability

Lens applies to all bubbles.
1 · Observer Invariance
Identical evidence must yield identical classification by independent observers.
If honest observers can’t agree from the same facts, the rules aren’t defined yet.
2 · Closure
Open loops accumulate instability; closed loops preserve stability.
Unfinished loops stack debt and eventually force instability.
