Where coherence shows up

Coherence shows up anywhere a system stays aligned under pressure.

  • Nature — stable ecosystems hold relationships without collapsing.
  • People — trust forms when words, actions, and outcomes match over time.
  • Groups — organizations stabilize when standards don’t change to excuse behavior.
  • Science — models stay coherent when assumptions and outputs don’t contradict.
