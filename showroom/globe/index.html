Condition Ownership

Each condition has one job.

Earth Ground, shoreline, and material seat.

Earth owns the foreground mass, the shoreline edge, the slope, and the grounded landing surface.

Water Horizon band, shimmer, and reflection.

Water owns the mid-band, reflective light, wave rhythm, and the transition between ground and distance.

Air Sky, light, haze, and depth.

Air owns the upper field, atmosphere, horizon haze, distant softness, and the breathing room above the waterline.
