ARCHCOIN

Introduction and handoff chamber

ARCHCOIN is a compact navigation surface built to hand you into the right financial room. The four coins are Contract, Receivable, Payable, and Allocation. Open the correct coin, inspect the active path, read the short briefing below the compass, and then continue into the room when ready.

Read More

This page is not an archive. It is an introduction and transfer point. The compass does the work: the outer field selects the domain, the active inner cluster selects the room, and the box beneath the compass gives the immediate explanation for the selected path. Contract handles agreement meaning and enforcement. Receivable handles incoming value and recognition. Payable handles outgoing obligation and execution. Allocation handles distribution and downstream assignment. Use this chamber to orient, inspect, and hand off.
