Lineage

Lineage is the traceable continuity of your standards across time, pressure, and audience.

It shows up when people can predict your behavior without negotiating for exceptions.

Lineage is not reputation. It is pattern memory formed by repeated exposure to the same constraints.

Lineage breaks when you become a different person depending on who benefits, who is watching, or what is at stake.

Lineage signature: fewer explanations, faster repair, and consistent boundaries across contexts.

Lineage is invariant classification across time: independent observers map the same evidence into the same structure.

It requires stable measurement rules. Without invariants, drift hides behind redefinitions and narrative substitutions.

Lineage breaks when control policy diverges from declared constraints under incentive pressure.

Lineage signature: bounded variance across contexts with stable observer agreement.

CANONICAL LAWS (COHERENCE)

  • Coherence Constraint Law — external coherence cannot exceed internal self-coherence.
  • Fragmentation Detection Law — misalignment between stated values, executed actions, and produced outcomes indicates loss of coherence.
