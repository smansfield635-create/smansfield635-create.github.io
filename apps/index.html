Apps

Apps are execution views into the quad-core system. They do not control the system. They reveal it.

Integrity

Admissibility Monitor

Observes what inputs are allowed, rejected, or deferred. Boundaries only. No persuasion.

Structure

Coherence Map

Reveals how accepted inputs organize over time. Highlights drift and stabilization.

Trajectory

Momentum Viewer

Displays direction and focus. Shows acceleration, deceleration, and priority pressure.

Vitality

Sustainability Gauge

Indicates whether the system can continue without burnout. Recovery is visible. Collapse is visible.
