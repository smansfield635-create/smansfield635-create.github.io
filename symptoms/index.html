Act I: Symptoms

Symptoms are signals. They tell you something is off — not why.

Common signals
  • Drift — plans, behavior, and outcomes slowly diverge.
  • Rule fatigue — boundaries exist but don’t constrain.
  • Promise decay — decisions are made without follow-through.
What this page is (and isn’t)

Confusion and instability are not failures. They are felt evidence that alignment has begun to slip.

Symptoms belong here. Causes come later.
