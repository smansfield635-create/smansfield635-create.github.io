HOW THIS SITE WORKS

Purpose Definition

This site is a structural interface. It does not sell belief. It binds every claim to bounded meaning, and it separates what is offered (claim) from what makes it verifiable (support).

Rule Anchor: Claims remain bounded. Support remains checkable. Navigation preserves state.

Fast Navigation

Use Prelude when you want a calm entry. Use Door when you want compass traversal. Use Enter when you want to jump directly into the hub and move by function.

Entry Rule: Prelude → Door → (N/E/S/W). Enter bypasses Prelude.

Claim vs Support

Claim means two things: a stated problem (a need) and a stated offer (a solution). Support means two things: assistance (services) and verification (systems). This site keeps them distinct on purpose.

Separation Rule: Products & Services = claim surface. Software & Systems = support surface.

Lenses Meaning

Formality changes wording and framing. Expression changes encoding posture (SSG/MSSG/BSSG). Chronology changes what you’re looking at (origin/current/trajectory). These toggles are not decoration; they change meaning.

Lens Rule: If a lens switch does not change content, it is a failure.
