Science · Math · Possibility

Separate what is real from what is ready.

Science Fusion is real. Controlled fusion requires plasma. Plasma must be heated, confined, stabilized, and sustained. Commercial plant-level electricity is not procurement-ready.
Math Gain types are not the same. Q_target, Q_plasma, and Q_plant must be separated. Target gain alone does not prove commercial electricity.
Possibility Businesses can prepare now. The path is not solar becomes fusion. The path is preparing load, storage, timing, contracts, site control, and infrastructure.
Math lock

Plant-level net electricity decides commercial viability.

Q_target Target gain. Fusion energy from target divided by energy delivered to target.
Q_plasma Plasma gain. Fusion thermal power divided by external plasma heating power.
Q_plant Plant gain. Net electric output divided by total facility energy input.
Commercial viability requires sustainable Q_plant greater than 1 after plasma heating, driver energy, confinement, cooling, pumping, fuel cycle, control systems, maintenance, conversion losses, and reset costs.
