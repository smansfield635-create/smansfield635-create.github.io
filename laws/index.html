PL3 · Laws · Rule Chamber

Open the law chamber before the system drifts.

Laws keeps authority, routes, instruments, runtime, generation standards, products, planets, and boundaries in order.

A living system cannot run on vibes alone. The Lawscape keeps the estate readable: what owns the page, what route it belongs to, what proof exists, what runtime is allowed, what generation is real, and what must not be overclaimed.

A system without law eventually becomes decoration.
Proof must outrank confidence when confidence gets loud.
The law chamber protects the promise from becoming a performance.

Open only the law you need.

Laws is compressed into nested layers: authority, route, proof, runtime, generation, product/planet separation, human boundary, routes, and receipt.

Russian doll mode
1 Authority law Root ownership stays explicit before design, runtime, or presentation claims. Open / close
Root law Every chamber needs a route, parent, construct class, and owner function before it can make a larger claim.
Entry law Compass orients, Door admits, Home stabilizes, Explore maps, and Laws governs. Do not collapse their jobs.
Surface law A page may present a claim only when the controlling source and route are not hidden behind style.
2 Route law Every chamber must know where it lives and where it sends the visitor next. Open / close
Canonical route Laws lives at /laws/ and remains a planetary rule chamber attached to the manor.
Backlink law Compass, Explore, Showroom, and Gauges must remain reachable so the rule layer does not become isolated.
No orphan law No public chamber should float without parent route, return route, and verification path.
3 Proof law Gauges, receipts, diagnostics, and visible routes outrank unsupported claims. Open / close
Gauge law Diagnostics stay visible before ornament. Claims must remain readable by Gauges and receipts.
Receipt law Every major renewal must leave a receipt describing what changed and what stayed untouched.
No false pass A visual page can look better without proving generation readiness. Those claims must stay separate.
4 Runtime law Motion must stay contained, readable, route-safe, and owned by the correct chamber. Open / close
Motion law Mounted motion belongs where it is authorized. Static route chambers should not load heavy render chains.
Containment law Runtime, renderer, instruments, and diagnostics must not leak into chambers that only need links.
Route-safe law Planetary visuals, product chambers, and proof routes must stay separable even when they link to one another.
5 Generation law Baseline, readiness, and generation labels must not blur into false completion. Open / close
Baseline law A baseline is a known accepted state. It is not a claim that all future generations are complete.
Readiness law Healthy enough is not the same as generation-ready. Marker health and generation readiness stay distinct.
Lock law When a generation is accepted, the page must say what is locked, what is reserved, and what is still future work.
6 Product and planet separation law Products stay products. Planetary constructs stay planetary constructs. Open / close
Product law ARCHCOIN, AAI, Five Flags, Education, and Nutrition are product chambers under Products.
Planet law Explore, Laws, Universe, Prelude, Frontier, and related world surfaces remain planetary constructs.
No collapse law A product may link to a planet and a planet may link to a product, but one should not pretend to be the other.
7 Human boundary law Support, explanation, preparation, and proof must not become reckless authority. Open / close
Support law AAI, education, nutrition, and other support chambers should prepare and organize without replacing qualified judgment.
Creative law Interactive scenes should use original, licensed, permissioned, public-domain, or parody-safe material.
Verification law Medical, legal, financial, safety, and official claims must mark what still needs professional or source verification.
The law chamber exists to keep the system useful without letting confidence outrun proof, route ownership, human boundaries, or generation truth.
8 Move through the rule map Control, route-map, product wing, proof surface, and diagnostics remain reachable. Open / close
Compass Return to the public command surface. Explore Return to the route-map planetary surface. Products Enter the product wing and product chambers. Showroom Inspect public proof surfaces and presentation lanes. Earth G1 Inspect the accepted Demo Universe baseline. Gauges Read route, runtime, parser, and diagnostic receipts.
