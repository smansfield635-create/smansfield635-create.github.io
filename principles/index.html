Human language

Tier 1 — What must hold

What is true stays true under pressure. What isn’t eventually breaks.

  • Honesty stabilizes decisions.
  • Boundaries reduce harm.
  • Inputs shape outcomes.
Tier 2 — How coherence is maintained
  • Make constraints explicit.
  • Use receipts for decisions.
  • Invalidate downstream when upstream changes.
Tier 3 — What you notice when coherence shifts
  • Drift: what you say/do/get stop matching.
  • Rule fatigue: boundaries exist but don’t bind behavior.
  • Promise decay: decisions happen without follow-through.
