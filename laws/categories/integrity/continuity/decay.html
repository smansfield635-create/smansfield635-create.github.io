Integrity does not collapse all at once. It decays quietly.

When correction latency increases, variance spreads and failures amplify across layers.

Decay accelerates under incentive misalignment and measurement drift.

Signature: rising entropy, rising exceptions, and delayed repair until brittle collapse.
