Quad-Core Engine

Structural execution model and cross-engine invariants.

System Overview

The Geodiametrics engine is not a workflow, dashboard, or optimization tool. It is a constraint-first execution surface composed of four irreducible cores. Multiple engines may exist simultaneously. Their interaction is governed by explicit, non-negotiable walls.

The Four Cores

Selection

Admissibility gate. Inputs must satisfy constraints to enter execution.

Resolution

Minimal structural completion. One admitted path resolves.

Commitment

Irreversible binding. No rollback once committed.

Continuation

Persistent execution state across time and reloads.

Cross-Engine Walls

Engines do not implicitly share state. Synchronization is explicit, scoped, and auditable. Structural walls prevent contamination, narrative leakage, and retroactive reinterpretation.
