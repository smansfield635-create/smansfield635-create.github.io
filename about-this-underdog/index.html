Voice

Finding the underdog is finding the voice.

This Underdog is the public-facing identity for the person who had been standing near the stage before recognizing that the stage was part of the path.

The work begins from the underdog position because the underdog knows pressure before applause. That pressure can become language. That language can become story. That story can become a room where other people recognize their own voice.
