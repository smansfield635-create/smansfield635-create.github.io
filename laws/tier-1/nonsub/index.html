1 No tradeoffs Meaning

Rule You can’t “make up for it” somewhere else.

When a requirement matters, failing it breaks the run—even if other parts look strong.

Non-Compensation Law

Strength in one required dimension does not compensate for violation in another required constraint.

2 No waiting Meaning

Rule Time doesn’t unlock permission.

Waiting is not completion. Progress requires the missing step, receipt, or constraint to be satisfied.

No-Timer Gating Law

Time passage cannot substitute for structural completion, receipt, or constraint satisfaction.
