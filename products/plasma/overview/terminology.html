Terminology

PLASMA · Definitions used on this product branch.

These terms keep the product pages stable and inspectable.

Receipt

A condition that proves a step is valid before moving forward.

Constraint

A non-negotiable rule bounding what counts as admissible.

Admissible step

A move that respects constraints and is supported by a receipt.

Drift

Hidden changes in assumptions/definitions that invalidate downstream work.

Operational vocabulary for constrained execution.

State

Validated configuration of evidence + constraints + choices.

Transition

State update gated by a receipt.

Invariant

Property that must remain true across transitions.

Invalidation

Downstream states become non-admissible when upstream assumptions change.
