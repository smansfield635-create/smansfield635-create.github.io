What keeps breaking in our systems?

When systems fail, it usually looks like one of these:

  • Drift: things stop lining up slowly.
  • Rules fail: boundaries exist but don’t constrain reliably.
  • Promises fail: decisions are made but outcomes don’t follow.

This is why we separate the story into three acts: Symptoms → Diagnosis → Execution.
