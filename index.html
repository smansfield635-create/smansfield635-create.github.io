Seven invariant constraints

  4. Invariant 4 — Constraints define admissibility.
  5. Invariant 5 — Verification gates reality.
  6. Invariant 6 — Confusion accelerates failure once incoherence exists.
  7. Invariant 7 — Receipts enable convergence.
