What the gauges mean.

The Manor is healthy when every room has one clear destination, every backlink returns correctly, every product opens into its proper chamber, and every public surface says where the visitor is standing.

The engineer wall tells us the numbers. This side tells us what to do with them.

Healthy The route law is working.

Products is behaving like a source chamber. The main product paths are no longer scattered across competing bodies.

Proof The public rooms are becoming places.

Showroom, Compass, Products, AAI, ESL, Nutrition, and ARCHCOIN now have stronger physical identities inside the Manor.

Priority Gauges is useful, not decorative.

This page tells us what is connected, what is stable, what needs inspection, and what should be patched next.

Complex Some rooms need interpretation.

Upper Room and ARCHCOIN contain more moving parts. They should be judged by live render, not by concept alone.

Next practical move

Inspect live render before patching. If a page visually fails, patch that page only. Do not rebuild route law unless a real backlink fails.
