The world idea

A world surface has three jobs.

It separates the map from the merchandise, connects public paths, and gives future rooms a place to emerge without crowding the Door.

Separate Keep products in Products.

The product engine has its own chamber. The Metaverse is the surrounding world, not a product list.

Connect Let paths meet cleanly.

Human paths, product paths, exploration paths, and future spaces can connect here without collapsing into one page.

Expand Hold future rooms without clutter.

The world surface can grow after the core routes are stable.
