Instrument Authority

How the Coherence Diagnostic works

The Technical Face explains the instrument, scoring structure, archetype system, pressure-break logic, and interpretation boundaries. Opening this face does not change, reset, or regenerate the assessment.

Structure Two intake anchors and ten scenarios The first two questions establish self-perception and claimed archetype blend. Ten real-world scenarios then test choices under pressure.
Weighting First move ×2, support move ×1 The first action carries greater weight. The secondary choice identifies the supporting pattern used next.
Subscales Six coherence dimensions Sequence, evidence, action, stability, correction, and contradiction resistance are calculated locally.
Boundary Reflective instrument, not clinical testing Experimental processing and personality-style outputs remain estimates and do not constitute official IQ or MBTI results.
