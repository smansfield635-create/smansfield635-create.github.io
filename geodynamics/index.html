Human translation

Confusion usually means signals stopped lining up. Coherence returns when reality becomes checkable.

  • Drift: words/actions/outcomes stop matching.
  • Pressure: reveals what’s real vs assumed.
  • Small corrections: prevent collapse.

Scientific language

Coherence(t) = 1 / (1 + Divergence(t))

Divergence = misalignment between declared and observed state.
