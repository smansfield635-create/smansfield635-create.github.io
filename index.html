What is coherence?

Coherence is the condition where signal, structure, pressure, and purpose begin to fit together.

The Minimum Principle Sd = min(Ed, Id, Vd)

Human meaning: coherence is determined by the weakest indispensable axis. Strength in one area does not compensate for failure in another required axis.

Practical meaning: when a person or system is trying to grow, the decisive question is often not “Where am I strongest?” but “Which required capacity is closest to failing?”
